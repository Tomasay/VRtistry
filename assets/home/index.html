The Party Crashers web app loads right in browser on any mobile device. No need to download an app, no account signup, no bs. Instantly jump right into the fun with your friends!

Minigames





Virtual Pursuit



A simple game of cat and mouse. The VR player must avoid the other players until time expires. Outnumbered it may be difficult, but they have a few tricks to help them!

Icy Ignition



The players are stranded on a sheet of ice and must try and hold on as the VR player throws fire down from the heavens.

VRtistry



Use a mannequin to pose and paint answers to prompts given to your friends. Show off your art skills by seeing how well others can guess your drawing correctly.
